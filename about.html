Mil's Kitchen

About Us

Mil's Kitchen was born in the heart of the tropical city of Rio de Janeiro. Our main character is our chef Mil. She's been cooking for the past 20 years and has many culinary background experiences under her magic hands. Our mission it's all about bringing to the world, or your table, a healthier gastronomic experience. You can have real food, real taste and real good. Mil's Kitchen, turns out dreams do come true.

Our Goals

We want to set you free from fast food but without losing its pros: taste and fast. We believe in the potential of a healthier community, and we want to bring real food and you altogether. Real food keeps the disease away, reduces pollution and makes you stronger. Our goal is to empower our custumers through our meals and by doing that we are able to take care of our world.
